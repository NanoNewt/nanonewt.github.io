Interests

Audiobooks

I really love audiobooks. I have listened to over 500. My favorite genres are Litrpgs, Urban Fantasy, and High Fantasy. Litrpgs are books set in a world with video game-like mechanics. To me the best part of books is exploring how magic and weird creatures could affect cultures, economies, and ecologies.

Magic The Gathering

Magic the gathering (MTG) is a collectable trading card game. I particularly like the command format where you and about 3 friends bring 100 card decks build around a special legendary creature card. I love the diversity of cards and strategies and exploiting rules interactions to do cool things.

Slay the Spire

Slay the Spire is my favorite video game. It is a roguelike deck builder where you start at the bottom of the spire with a basic deck. As you fight through the monsters toward the top of the spire you improve your deck by adding cards that are good or synergistic, removing bad card, and upgrading cards.
